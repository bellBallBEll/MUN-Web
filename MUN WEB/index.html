About JCID MUN II

JCID MUN II is a student-led Model United Nations conference designed for middle and high school delegates. Our goal is to build leadership, research, and public speaking skills while fostering respect for diverse perspectives.

Conference theme

“Global Cooperation, Local Change” explores how international policy can support community-level solutions for climate, education and human rights.

Who can join

Open to all students from grade 7 to 12. Beginners and experienced delegates are welcome, with coaching sessions available before the conference.

Committees

UN General Assembly

Debate climate action and sustainable development goals through a global lens.

UNESCO

Discuss education, science, and cultural cooperation in a rapidly changing world.

Security Council

Address peacekeeping, conflict prevention, and diplomatic crisis management.
