About JCID MUN II

JCIDMUN is more than just a platform for debate. JCIDMUN is a place where the thinkers, writers, and leaders of tomorrow come together to discuss how to truly make a difference. Our beginner friendly conference brings students from very diverse backgrounds together, allowing them to work together to solve with the many issues that plague our world. Through competitive and respectful debate, delegates at JCIDMUN manage international relations, draft actionable solutions, and build connections with those they work with (or against!). Whether you are a veteran debater or a first timer, JCIDMUN provides a space where every voice is heard.

Conference theme

Unity with Purpose

In an ironically interconnected and disjoint world, gathering together is not enough. We have to unite with both direction and intent. The theme for this year’s conference, Unity with Purpose, challenges delegates to go further than symbolic consensus and start making meaningful and coordinated actions. True unity isn’t about eradicating disagreement, but is about aligning every nation’s diverse strengths toward a common goal. As you enter each committee, you should think about not just what can we agree on, but what can we achieve together? Each and every resolution you draft will be a testament to what happens when human collaborate towards a clear and shared goal. This conference should be a reminder that the most powerful alliances are created not in uniformity, but in united purpose.
