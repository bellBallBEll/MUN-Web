Security Council

The Privatization of Warfare, Private Military Companies

The Security Council will evaluate the ongoing conflict, humanitarian emergencies, and diplomatic pathways to a sustainable ceasefire. Delegates will debate sanctions, aid corridors, and international peacekeeping cooperation.

  • Humanitarian assistance and refugee protection.
  • Conflict de-escalation and ceasefire enforcement.
  • Coordination with regional and UN partners.

The Escalating Tension in the Sahel Region

Delegates will work on crafting international frameworks to prevent cyber attacks, secure critical infrastructure, and establish norms for responsible state behavior in cyberspace.
